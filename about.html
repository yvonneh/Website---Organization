About Leadership Development

Building Rural Capacity through Leadership Development

Rural communities throughout our region have knowledgeable and skilled individual and organizational leaders who are strongly networked and make a difference in their own organizations and communities across the Pacific Northwest. We believe that effective, diverse, and collaborative leadership is an essential component of vital rural communities. We also believe that leadership can be an intentional and learned skill.

RDI has delivered our flagship Ford Institute Leadership Program in more than 350 communities, serving nearly 90 rural hubs across the Pacific Northwest with over 9,000 rural leadership graduates. We also deliver the Sherwood Trust Community Leadership Program and Facilitation Training, and we offer leadership services cusomized to fit the your community's needs.
